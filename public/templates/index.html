FEH Proximity Demo

This is a mock website playing around with Fire Emblem Heroes (FEH) units and the notion of proximity. I hope we (I) can get things moving. The operations planned to be implemented so far are UNIT INTERPOLATION and NEAREST NEIGHBORS.

Unit Interpolation

Draws a line between two units based on their [HP, ATK, SPD, DEF, RES] stats, treated as a 5-dimensional vector. The nearest unit to each 1/10-th of the line is then found and returned.
